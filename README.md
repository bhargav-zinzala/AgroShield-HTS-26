# 🌱 AgriShield AI  
AI-Based Crop Health Monitoring using Satellite Data

AgriShield AI is a smart agriculture solution built during **Hack The Spring 2026 (Hack.X)** at Government Engineering College, Gandhinagar.

The project focuses on helping farmers monitor crop health using **satellite imagery and environmental data**. By analyzing crop conditions in real-time, farmers can detect problems early and take timely actions.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Agriculture is a backbone of the Indian economy, yet many farmers face challenges such as:

- Crop diseases
- Pest attacks
- Unpredictable weather
- Lack of real-time crop monitoring
- Limited access to modern technology

Most farmers only discover crop damage **after it has already spread**, causing major losses.

---

## 💡 Solution

**AgriShield AI** uses **satellite imagery and data analysis** to monitor crop health and provide farmers with insights about their crops.

The system helps farmers:

- Monitor crop conditions
- Detect potential crop diseases
- Identify pest-related issues
- Receive real-time crop updates
- Make better farming decisions
